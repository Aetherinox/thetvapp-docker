TheTvApp for Docker
This page displays your most recent copies of TheTvApp's .m3u8 playlist and .xml EPG guide data. Right-click each file, select Copy Link and paste the URLs within your IPTV apps such as Jellyfin. The .m3u8 and .m3u8.gz are identical guide lists, but the .xml.gz is compressed and will import into your IPTV application much faster.
